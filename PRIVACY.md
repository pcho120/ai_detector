# Privacy Policy

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your documents. Our application is designed with a strict no-retention policy to protect your academic work.

## Data Collection & Retention

- **Immediate Deletion**: All uploaded files (`.docx`, `.doc`) and extracted text are held in temporary memory or temporary system storage and are deleted immediately after the analysis is complete.
- **No Persistent Storage**: We do not maintain a database of essays, user profiles, or analysis history.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use persistent cookies, analytics, or third-party tracking scripts to monitor your activity.

## Document Processing

- **English Only**: Our analysis pipeline is configured for English-language documents only.
- **External Analysis**: To perform the AI risk assessment, your document's text is sent to our trusted third-party detection provider (Sapling) via an encrypted connection.
- **Limits**: We enforce a 5 MB file size limit and a 100,000-character text limit to ensure secure and efficient processing.

## Security

- **Encryption**: All communications between your browser and our server, and between our server and the detection provider, are encrypted via TLS.
- **Secret Management**: API keys and environment variables are strictly managed on the server and are never exposed to the client-side bundle.

## Disclaimer

- **Risk Assessment**: The results provided are an "AI-like phrasing risk review," not a definitive proof of origin. Detection scores are probabilistic and should be used as one of many inputs for academic review.
- **No Evasion**: This tool is designed to help you improve your writing's authenticity and specificity. We do not provide instructions or strategies to evade detection tools.
- **No Guarantees**: While we strive for accuracy, the results are for informational purposes and carry no guarantee of 100% accuracy or outcome in academic assessments.
